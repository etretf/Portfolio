This Quiet

Acrylic on canvas

My work is an Abstract monochromatic painting that features clashing lines of movement with contrast between floating strokes of white and dark pools of black. My intention was to create a piece with building and releasing tension through the changes in colour, stroke intensity, and style in sections. The difference between the flowing sections and the erratic sections in the blue monochrome palette can appear like thrashing waves.
